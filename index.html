Managing Your Digital Identity

Every photo, comment, and post you make contributes to your digital identity. Employers, schools, and strangers can make opinions based on what you share. Managing your digital identity means being aware of how your actions affect your reputation, privacy, and security

What is a digital footprint

Your activity online both in what you post and what others share about you

Why it matters

A strong positive digital identity can open doors while a careless one can close them and potentially cause security and privacy threats
